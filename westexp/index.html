Introduction

Join me to learn of the historic events that took place during the 19th century of America focusing around the movement to the western side of the U.S. Learn how the west was settled, how natives got to where they are are today and why settlers moved westward. Explore and discover how the choices of the American people and government in the past affected the people we know as Native Americans.
