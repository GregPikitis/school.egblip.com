David the Doctor
For my character analysis, my group and I focused on David, a doctor who Toni met while working to be a nun. He left the monastary to marry her. Though this was controversial, David and Toni were happy with their decision and later worked to prevent people from being treated unfairly.

For the physical aspect of the project, we created a poster with a drawing of David on it, and chose quotes that we felt demonstrated his character. We wrote these quotes next to the body part that associated with them, in English and in Spanish. We presented this poster infront of the class.
