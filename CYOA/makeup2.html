Choose Your Own Adventure:
Life at Pathways

Made by Emelia Blankenship

You succeed in finding your friends locker and go to the bathroom to apply her makeup. Somehow, these events lead to much more lateness than would be expected of just transit time. You go back to your classroom but your teacher is not pleased and gives you lunch detention for stalling in the hallways excessively. This cruel punishment results in a plumet in your self esteem, and you resort to killing yourself, because how could you live with this guilt?
