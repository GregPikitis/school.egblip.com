Life at Pathways

Made by Emelia Blankenship

He hears your blunt response and shakes his head, you two have been through this before. He continues to tell you that you must either put it away or he will take it, but you sharply respond by saying, "There is no way in hell you are taking it". He continues to blab on and you ignore him and continue with your class work. Eventually he gives up and leaves you be, perhaps he was summoned by his radio or had other students to follow, you honestly have no clue. This gives you a sense of ego and this changes you for life. This confidence allows you to fight for your freedoms and the freedoms of those around you, and you are highly praised. You go down in history as someone who refuses to back down!
